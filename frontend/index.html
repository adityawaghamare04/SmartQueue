How SmartQueue works

Six simple steps from scan to service — no app, no login required.

01
📷

Scan the QR Code

Click "Join Queue" on the home page. Point your camera at the QR code displayed by the admin to get started instantly.

02
📋

Fill Your Details

Enter your name, phone number, age, and city. Quick one-time form — no account or password needed.

03
✅

Join the Queue

Hit "Join Queue" and you're instantly added. You'll see your position number and estimated wait time right away.

04
📡

Track Live Status

Watch the queue move in real-time. See who's being served, how many people are ahead, and your updated wait time.

05
🧑‍💼

Admin Manages Queue

Admin marks each user as Complete or Absent from the panel. The next person moves up automatically in real-time.

06
🎉

Get Served

When it's your turn, your name appears at the top as "Now Serving". Walk in and get served — no confusion, no chaos.
